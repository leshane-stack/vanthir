Vanthir

Property intelligence for Miami-Dade County.

For any parcel, Vanthir assembles the owner of record, recorded sale history, and a multi-year assessed-value trajectory — each fact dated and sourced to the Miami-Dade Property Appraiser. It's a record of what the public county data says about a property over time, not a listings site or an estimate.
